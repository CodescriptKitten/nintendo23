Nintendo's central focus is the research, development, production, and distribution of entertainment products—primarily video game software and hardware and card games. Its main markets are Japan, America, and Europe, and more than 70% of its total sales come from the latter two territories. As of 2022, Nintendo has sold more than 5.4 billion video games and over 800 million hardware units.

The total capital of Nintendo today amounts to 10,065,400,000 yen or 76,573,033.77 USD. With making around 1,600,000 yen or 12,172.08 of net sales this year.
